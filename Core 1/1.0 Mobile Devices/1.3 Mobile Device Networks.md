## Cellular networks
- **Mobile devices**
	- "Cell" phones
- **Separate land into "cells"**
	- Antenna coverages a cell with certain frequencies
- **Enable or disable voice and/or data**
	- Airplane mode disables all cellular networks
	- Could also disable data but keep voice
